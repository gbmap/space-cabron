Space Cabrót is a rythmic shoot-em-up with procedurally generated music and synths. The player needs to fire the correct colors on beat to defeat enemies.

Story:
Having the peach he got for free at the ✝👁 Manufacturing Plant™ stolen from the cybernetic chickens, Space Cabrót sets out for revenge and reclaiming his beloved possession. Will he be able to achieve his goal? I don't know, you tell me.

Playtime: Approximately 20 minutes (Perfect run).
